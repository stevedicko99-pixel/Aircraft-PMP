# Aircraft Maintenance Predictive - Website Structure & Content

## Website Architecture

### Main Pages
1. **Home** - Landing page with hero section and feature highlights
2. **Learning Hub** - Educational resources on aerospace engineering, aircraft maintenance, and AI
3. **News & Updates** - Latest news from aerospace industry, universities, and research
4. **Blogs** - In-depth articles on aeronautical engineering, predictive maintenance, AI applications
5. **Community** - Connect with aerospace engineering enthusiasts, forums, and networking
6. **Resources** - Tools, documentation, and reference materials
7. **About** - Information about the platform and mission

### Navigation Structure
- **Top Navigation:** Logo, Home, Learning Hub, News, Blogs, Community, Resources, About
- **Footer:** Quick links, social media, contact, newsletter signup

## Design Elements

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Aerospace Blue (#0B3D91)
- **Accent:** Electric Cyan (#00D9FF)
- **Neutral:** Charcoal (#1A1A1A), Light Gray (#F5F5F5)
- **Text:** Dark gray on light backgrounds, light gray on dark backgrounds

### Typography
- **Display Font:** Sora Bold (geometric, technical)
- **Heading Font:** Sora SemiBold
- **Body Font:** Inter Regular
- **Accent Font:** Sora Medium

### Visual Assets
- Hero blueprint background
- AI predictive maintenance visualization
- Aerospace community illustration
- Geometric patterns for sections
- Icons for categories and features
- Aircraft silhouettes as design elements

### Layout Principles
- Asymmetric layouts with diagonal elements
- Generous whitespace for clarity
- Geometric shapes and lines suggesting motion
- Staggered card layouts for content
- Responsive design for mobile, tablet, desktop
